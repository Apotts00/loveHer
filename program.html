The Mentorship Experience

A guided mentorship program helping girls become confident, capable, and community-minded.

Program Pillars

BecomeHer

Identity, confidence, self-worth, self-respect, and personal growth.

LeadHer

Leadership, goal-setting, communication, decision-making, and future planning.

EmpowHer

Life skills, financial literacy, independence, self-advocacy, and resilience.

Sample 6-Week Program

Week 1

Who Am I?

Confidence, identity, and self-worth.

Week 2

My Strengths

Recognizing gifts, talents, and personal power.

Week 3

My Voice Matters

Communication, boundaries, and self-advocacy.

Week 4

Money Moves

Financial literacy, saving, budgeting, and choices.

Week 5

LeadHer

Leadership, goals, and future vision.

Week 6

EmpowHer Celebration

Reflection, sisterhood, and celebrating growth.
