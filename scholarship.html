Lady Love Empowerment Scholarship

Supporting young women who lead, give back, and rise with purpose.

About the Scholarship

The Lady Love Empowerment Scholarship supports young women who demonstrate resilience, leadership, community involvement, and a commitment to positive life choices.

This scholarship is an extension of the LoveHer mission and helps remind young women that their dreams are worthy of support.

Scholarship Snapshot

  • $500 annual award
  • Female high school seniors
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA
  • Attending a 2-year or 4-year college
  • Community involvement essay required

Support the Scholarship

Sponsors and community partners help provide financial support and encouragement to deserving young women as they take the next step in their education.
